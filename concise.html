Addendum 📓

1 - Several nations and territories that were part of the British Empire appeared together in a section entitled "British Empire" from 1924 to 1931. After 1931, these countries were reordered by country name.

2 - A few nations were renamed, either by the nation itself or the Yearbook publishers, during the period in which the Armaments Year Books were published and thus appeared in different orders of the Year Book. Iran was called Persia before 1935. The Kindgom of Serbia, Croatia and Slovenia became Yugoslavia in 1930. Thailand was called Siam before 1939.
